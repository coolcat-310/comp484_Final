Welcome to Motion Fun

Motion Fun

Motion fun is an easy Blender™ add-on that will ignite your creative freedom. Let your imagination run wild with a great way to capture movement and orientation.
 

Learn More

Free & Open Source

Harnessing the power of open source API, let your ideas overflow with creativity. As a free open source creative suite, motion fun is a natural solution for tracking 3D motion and orientation.
